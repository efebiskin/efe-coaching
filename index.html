FAQ

Good questions.

Do I need to be in good shape to start?

No. Every program starts where you are — total beginner or returning after a break. We scale it to you.

Online or in-person — which is better?

Both work. In-person is best for hands-on technique; online gives you full programming and support anywhere. Not sure? The free consult sorts it out.

Do I need a gym?

For online coaching we program around what you have — full gym, home setup, or minimal equipment.

How fast will I see results?

Most people feel stronger and more consistent within a few weeks. Real, lasting change is a season, not a week — and we track it the whole way.
